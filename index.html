Skiing in Summit County

Home About

Every year, thousands of tourists flock to Summit County for the incredible snow and scenery. In total there are 7 main resorts in the area. However, if you want a truly unique ski experience with less crowds and unbelievable terrain, check out this web site. Consider it your survival guide! Happy skiing!

Summit County average snowfall is 175 inches a year and we are blessed with 245 sunny days! Situated on picturesque Lake Dillon, the local here believe you come for the winter, and stay for summer.

There are many choices for fun and adventure, but these three have unique features that make them locals favs.

  • A Basin
  • Keystone
